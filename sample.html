📸 Golden Hour Photography

Capture the Perfect Moment

Pro Tips

1

Best Time to Shoot

The golden hour occurs approximately 1 hour before sunset.

2

Face the Light

Position yourself with the sun behind or to the side for that warm, glowing effect.

3

Silhouettes & Backlighting

For dramatic photos, try shooting directly into the sun for stunning silhouettes.

4

Camera Settings

Lower ISO (100-400), wider aperture (f/1.8-f/4), and adjust white balance to Cloudy or Shade.
